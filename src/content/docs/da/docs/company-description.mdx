Crowdin is a cloud-based localization management software that helps teams go global and stay agile.

## Om Crowdin

Crowdin is a product-based company founded in 2009 and has since grown to 3M+ user accounts.

Crowdin-software er en lokaliseringshåndteringsløsning til smidige teams. We empower companies of all shapes and sizes to grow by reaching people who speak different languages. Millions of users from around the world have registered to deliver their websites, mobile apps, games, documents, and other content in the language of their customers.

Crowdins hensigt er at stille de seneste teknologiløsninger til rådighed, som gør oversættelse og lokalisering så let som muligt.
